# Introducing Pharmacy Inventory v2.1

<!-- TODO: Replace with full article (300–500 words) -->

Running a district pharmacy without real-time stock visibility is like navigating at night without headlights. You know you're moving, but you can't see what's coming until it's right in front of you.

Pharmacy Inventory v2.1 started as a personal tool — a spreadsheet I built in 2023 to track stock levels across the Mamfe health district. Over 18 months, it was refined through actual use, broken by real conditions, and rebuilt every time it failed.

The result: a tool that does one thing extremely well. It tells you, at a glance, which drugs are below safety stock, which are likely to run out before the next resupply, and which orders are overdue.
